Our Story

From humble beginnings to Charlotte's favorite vegetarian destination

Rooted in Charlotte

VivaVeggie began in 2015 when three friends with a passion for plant-based cooking decided to transform Charlotte's dining scene.

Starting as a small weekend farmer's market stall, we quickly grew a devoted following for our creative vegetarian dishes made with locally-sourced ingredients.

Two years later, we opened our first brick-and-mortar location in the heart of Charlotte. Today, we're proud to offer a full dining experience that celebrates the bounty of local farms while proving that vegetarian cuisine can be both nutritious and delicious.

Meet Our Team

The passionate people behind our farm-to-table mission

Our Chefs

Chef Maria Rodriguez

Executive Chef

With over 15 years of experience in vegetarian cuisine, Maria brings creative vision and technical expertise to every dish. Trained at the Culinary Institute of America, she specializes in transforming traditional dishes into plant-based masterpieces.

"The beauty of plants speaks for itself. My job is just to listen."

Chef James Kim

Pastry Chef

James revolutionizes plant-based desserts with innovative techniques and unexpected flavor combinations. His creations prove that vegan desserts can be just as decadent and satisfying as traditional ones.

"Nature offers the perfect sweetness. We don't need to look further."

Chef Amelia Johnson

Sous Chef

Amelia brings global inspiration to our menu, drawing from her travels across Asia, Europe, and South America. She has a talent for creating bold, complex flavors using simple, sustainable ingredients.

"Cooking with plants connects us to the earth and to each other."

Chef David Ortiz

Farm Liaison & Menu Developer

David works directly with our farm partners to develop seasonal menus that highlight the best of what's growing. His background in both farming and culinary arts makes him the perfect bridge between field and plate.

"The best flavors come from vegetables harvested that same morning."

Our Farmers

Robert Wilson

Green Valley Farm (15 miles)

Robert's family has been farming the same land for four generations. His 20-acre farm specializes in heirloom tomatoes, peppers, and root vegetables grown using regenerative agriculture techniques.

Supplies our seasonal vegetables

Sarah & Michael Chen

Sunrise Orchards (28 miles)

This husband-and-wife team left corporate careers to start their organic orchard. They grow over 15 varieties of apples, pears, and stone fruits using sustainable practices that promote biodiversity.

Supplies our fruits for desserts

Elena Diaz

Wildflower Fields (8 miles)

Elena's urban farm specializes in microgreens, herbs, and edible flowers. Her innovative vertical farming techniques allow her to grow year-round while minimizing water usage and maximizing space.

Supplies our herbs and microgreens
